    TERMS & CONDITIONS

    1. Discussion
    Please contact me on Discord, Instagram, or Twitter. Describe in detail what you would like to commission or send reference pictures of what you'd like.

    2. Sketching Phase
    I will start a sketch.
    At this stage:
    ♡ Any changes can be made.

    3. Payment
    When the sketch is confirmed, I will send you an invoice via PayPal.
    For commissions over $80, you may choose to pay 50% of the price first, then pay the rest later. Just be sure to inform me of your preferred method of pay.

    4. Lineart Phase
    I will start on the lineart.
    At this stage:
    ♡ Major changes (posing, composition) will require an additional fee.
    ♡ Minor changes (accessory, details) are still allowed.

    5. Colouring Phase
    After the lineart is confirmed, I will move on to colouring.
    At this stage:
    ♡ I will refuse to make major changes.
    ♡ Minor changes will require an additional fee.
    ♡ Colour adjustments are allowed.

    6. Completion
    After the final drawing is approved, I will send an email containing the final drawing.
    ♡ If 100% of the payment was made beforehand, and no additional fees were charged, I will send the final product after final confirmation.
    ♡ If 100% of the payment was made beforehand, but additional fees were charged from changes, I will ask for those fees to be paid before sending the final product.
    ♡ If 50% of the payment was made beforehand, I will ask for the rest of the payment (including additional fees if changes were made to the drawing) before I send the final product.
